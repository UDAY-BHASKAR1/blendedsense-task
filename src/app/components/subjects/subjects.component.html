Frequently Asked Questions

When Will I see my Assets?

One week after the Sweep, you will receive a post production checkpoint where you can deliver any feedback. A week later you will receive your final assets delivered in the Blended Sense platform!

What is a post-production checkpoint?

This is a first look at your assets, and time to give feedback in order to get the final results you want. You will have 2-3 business days to send over any revisions so we can deliver your finished assets on time.

When will I receive my final product / assets?

2 weeks post Sweep.
